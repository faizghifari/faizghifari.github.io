Bismillah.

*Which one do i prefer, light or dark?*

*I’ll choose dark.*

*But it doesn’t mean that I meant to be bad. Where dark is a theme that strongly related with everything that considered ‘not good’.*

*I choose dark, not because of its emptiness.*

*Nor because of its black all-around or because of the absence of lights.*

*It quites the opposite.*

*I like darkness, and I choose it, because of ****lights****.*

*Why? Simple enough*

*In the middle of darkness, even the least-shining light source will look so bright*

*When nights are long and empty, the moon and stars will voluntarily appears, but if and only if there are no other light sources that deemed their shines*

*The dark sky, embraces cute little star that so far away, to be brave and glows, try to lighting up the entire universe even though just a little dot inside our gloomy eyes*

*But sometimes, it feels lonely, inside a quiet place where there are no lights to guide, no sound to hear, no colors to imagine, and no one, but yourself*

*When our minds start thinking crossed the line. Obscuring reality from sweet dreams and nightmares.*

*In the bleak of nights*, *darkness appears,* *from the mid of emptiness,* *accompanying all tired souls,* *after a long-exhausting noon*

*It never mean to frighten*, *nor interfere your rest,* *all it wants is to hold you*, *guides you to pray,* *and nothing but cares*

*while outside,* *stars and moon,* *while inside,* *little light sleeper*, *shine bright to keep* *you safe*

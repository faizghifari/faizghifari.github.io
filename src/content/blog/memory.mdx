*2:15 P.M*. *August, 19th*

*Heyyy~~!!*

*How’s your holiday? I heard you went abroad this time. Where is that, Malaysia? A~h, I envy you so much, you know?*

*How about your head? Was that headache still come out of a sudden? Well, I hope it wasn’t*.

*I just wanna tell you that I’m currently stressed out, by those people. You know whose I’m talking about, right? They’re just… idk, make me feel so uncomfortable and guilty while I didn’t remember anything I did wrong. Every time I was passing by*, *they just came and shouted at me.* “*Hypocrite!*” *they said.*

*I knew, this happen because of that freaking creatures, slandered over me for all bad things I’ve never done. And spread the fake news to all ears that always love gossip* *like that.*

*Seriously mate, I can’t even focus on myself for the upcoming midterm* *exam*. *How could I get a good score in this kind of situation? I’ve tried to ignore them, but they became even more violent. I’ve tried to be kind, but that also didn’t work.*

*Haaaaaaah, I think I was going crazy because of this.*

*What I think is, if they that are close even get harsh on me*, *then how about you and your friends? Are you mad at me? Hate me? Like just the way they did?*

*Even though, I believe you aren’t*.

*I’m really scared right now*. *I’m sad, angry, afraid, etc.* *Whereas in the past they were people who were very close to me*.

*Could you… help me, this time? Just tell me what you’re thinking.* *But yeah, it’s up to you. I know you may shocked receiving this message out of the blue. But the important thing is, just by having you listen to all of this nonsense, makes me a bit relieved.*

*I guess it’s enough. Sorry for disturbing you!*

*Thanks!*

*P.S. You know what? My head hurts so much while writing all of this, I guess it feels just like yours*

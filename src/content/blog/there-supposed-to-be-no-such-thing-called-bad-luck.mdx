Bismillah.

.

.

.

.

“*Why?”*

.

*It’s just, a bit of my worries that came on my face.*

*A fear to be left.* *A fear to fail. A fear of*…

*losing.*

*Sometimes it just felt so real.*  *When the time comes, and I’m not ready to face the truth.*

.

“*Nothing.”*

.

*I realize.*

*When something appears in front of ourselves and everything we see just the bad?*

*Is that why Allah create a pair of eyes for us?* *To always see the worst?* *To see everything we don’t have?* *To ignore all the thing we already had?*

.

*“Are you OK?*“

.

*Oh, bad luck.*

*I love bad luck.* *Not in any moment, but, I’m just glad to have experience on it.*

.

*Like i said.* *It’s similar with puzzle.*

*When it comes in 2 opposite pieces, it’s hard to see the whole thing.* *You need to be patient, look for middle pieces, and arrange it all correctly.*

*But, hey,*

*sometimes we’re not eager enough to play this game*, *and not interested enough to do something more than judg*e *the incoming disaster.*

.

*“Yeah, I am OK.”*

.

*And here comes the problem.*

*It’s just us, who roughly thinking by all the negativity above the surface.  Accusing all things that happened as a stroke of nightmares, felt ourselves like the unlucky ones.*

*While we hesitate to dig down a bit in order to find a hidden treasure that Allah has prepared it for us.  And not patient enough to wait and see how a bitter seed grows into a piece of fruit that sweet.*

*We’re just too fast to judge ourselves unblessed, even when we knew we shouldn’t*.

.

*“Is it an answer?*“

.

*Yeah, because,*

***there supposed to be no such thing called bad luck.***

*It’s just us, who close one of our eyes, and didn’t want to wait long enough to realize,*

*maybe*, *it’s not a bad luck.*

.

*“Maybe.”*

.

*It’s just a blessing that not be found*,

*and we didn’t know it.*

*But Allah always knew.*

.

“*I know you don’t.”*

.

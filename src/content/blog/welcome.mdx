Welcome to my blog! This is where I'll share thoughts on research, technology, and occasionally life updates.

## What to Expect

I plan to write about:

1. **Research updates** — progress on my current projects, paper acceptances, and interesting findings
2. **Technical deep-dives** — code-friendly posts about NLP, LLMs, and machine learning
3. **Life notes** — occasional reflections on life as a researcher in South Korea

## Why a Blog?

I've always enjoyed writing as a way to think through ideas. This blog is my space to share thoughts that don't quite fit into academic papers — early ideas, reflections, tutorials, and life updates.
